Mattia Almansi

Physical Oceanographer

National Oceanography Centre Southampton

Hi there! I’m a physical oceanographer at the National Oceanography Centre in Southampton. I’m primarily interested in the physical processes governing the overflows of dense water between ocean basins. To address the targets of my research I run ocean general circulation models and I combine the numerical solutions with available observations.

During my PhD I studied the dynamics controlling the ocean circulation in the vicinity of Denmark Strait, advised by Prof. Tom Haine.
